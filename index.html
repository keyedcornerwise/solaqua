About

SolarAqua has been established in Ireland by our professional team with more than 10 years experience in designing solar thermal heating systems for the Irish climate. Solar tube panels and other solar system components from SolarAqua are among the best solar products available on a nationwide & worldwide scale. We are an authorised installing solar company for the Shentai Solar Group range of solar products. Shentai Solar Group are one of the largest manufacturers of solar thermal evacuated tubes collectors and ancillary solar components in the solar thermal market.
